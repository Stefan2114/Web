TO OUR NEWLY ADMITTED BRUINS,

What does it mean to be accepted to UCLA? Let us try to put it into words ...

  • U is for understanding that you’re capable of greatness and going for it! Your commitment, focus and determination are inspiring.
  • C is for congratulations! Celebrate this moment and everything you did to get here. We can’t wait to help you shape the future of your dreams.
  • L is for the long distance you’ve come, the hurdles you’ve conquered, and the dynamic life full of collaboration, community and innovative thinking that you’re sure to lead as a Bruin.
  • A is for the appreciation we feel, both for you and for all those in your life who’ve offered support and guidance.

Now it’s time to savor this moment — your moment! And know that we are thrilled to welcome you to the Bruin family.

Sincerely,
UCLA
